SAUSAGE ROLL

Description

A sausage roll is a savoury pastry meal, popular in current and former Commonwealth nations, consisting of sausage meat wrapped in puff pastry. Sausage rolls are sold at retail outlets and are also available from bakeries as a take-away food. This meal is quite easy to make at the comfort of your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Ready-rolled puff pastry - for extra speediness
  • 1 egg
  • Pork sausages (or use sausage meat)

Steps

  1. Remove the skins from the sausages and unroll the pastry.
  2. Slice the pastry in half length-ways, then line up the sausages along the middle of both lengths pastry strips - squish the sausage meat together so it's even.
  3. Brush the top length of each of the two pastry pieces with egg wash and roll up, then press the seams shut using a fork.
  4. Brush the tops with egg wash and cut into mini sausage rolls. Prick the top of each one with the tip of a sharp knife.
