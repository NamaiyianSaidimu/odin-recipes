Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Chicken alfredo is an all-time classic mealâ€”a creamy white sauce, long strands of pasta mixed with tender pieces of lean protein. For this recipe, it is advisable to cut the meat for quicker cooking and use a generous amount of seasonings for maximum flavor. If you're a fan of some delicious gourmet Italian dinner, then you're in the right place.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 1/2 cups of whole milk
  • 8oz fettuccine
  • 2 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan
  • freshly chopped parsley for garnishing

Steps

  1. Heat some oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook until golden and cooked through, 8 minutes per side. Let rest 10 minutes, then slice
  2. Add milk, broth, and garlic to skillet. Season with salt and pepper and bring to a simmer. Add fettuccine, stirring frequently for about 3 minutes. Let cook until al dente, 8 minutes more
  3. Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan until combined. Simmer until sauce thickens
  4. Remove from heat and stir in sliced chicken. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
